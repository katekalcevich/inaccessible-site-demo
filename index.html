Pablo G.

Pablo is an internationally recognized yoga teacher who blends physical strength with an authentic inquiry into the nature of being to bring the ancient teachings of wisdom into the Bikram practice.

सतो मा सद्गमय ।
तमसो मा ज्योतिर्गमय ।
मृत्योर्माऽमृतं गमय ॥
From falsehood lead me to truth,
From darkness lead me to light,
From death lead me to immortality.
